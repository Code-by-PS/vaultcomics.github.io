About Us

Welcome to Vault Comics, your home for classic comic treasures. Based in the heart of Manchester, we collect and sell vintage comics from legendary publishers like DC and Marvel.

Whether you're hunting down rare editions or just starting your collection, VaultComics is here to help. We’re passionate about preserving the golden age of comics and sharing it with fans like you.

Looking to sell your own collection? Get in touch — we also buy vintage comics and love hearing from fellow collectors.
